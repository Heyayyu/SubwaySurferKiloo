BECOME A SUBWAY SURFING LEGEND

Well start from the ground up. See those striped construction signs? Theyre sturdier than they look. You can jump over the short ones and roll under the tall ones. By pressing down, you can come rocketing back to the ground and even roll under a high obstacle immediately after landing. Its a super important skill, so make sure you master it! Also, dont forget that you can control Jakes (and all the other characters) movement in the air. If you need to dash to another lane while in midair, you can! Finally, make sure you are always looking as far ahead as you can. The farther you can see, the more time you will have to react to whats coming.
